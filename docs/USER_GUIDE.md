# AKILIMO Mobile — User Guide

---

## Getting Started

### First Launch — Onboarding

AKILIMO guides you through a one-time setup the first time you open the app. Each step collects information needed to generate accurate recommendations for your farm.

**Steps:**

1. **Language** — Choose English, Kiswahili, or Kinyarwanda. The app will restart to apply your language.
2. **Disclaimer** — Read and accept the agronomic disclaimer.
3. **Terms** — Read and accept the terms of use.
4. **Your Name** — Enter your first and last name.
5. **Country** — Select your country (Nigeria, Tanzania, Ghana, Rwanda, or Burundi).
6. **Location** — Allow GPS access or tap "Pick on map" to set your farm location.
7. **Area Unit** — Select how you measure your land (Acre, Hectare, Are, or m²).
8. **Planting Dates** — Set your planting date and expected harvest date.
9. **Tillage** — Select your tillage method and enter associated costs.
10. **Investment** — Choose how much you plan to invest in inputs.
11. **Summary** — Review your information and submit to receive recommendations.

## Main Flows

### Getting a Fertilizer Recommendation

1. Complete onboarding (or open the app if already set up).
2. Tap the crop type you want a recommendation for (cassava, maize, sweet potato).
3. Enter market prices for your produce.
4. Enter your yield target or select from benchmarks.
5. Tap **Get Recommendation**.
6. Review the recommendation: fertilizer type, quantity per unit area, expected ROI.

### Changing Your Language

1. Open the app.
2. Tap the **settings icon** (gear/FAB) on the main screen.
3. In Settings, tap the **Language** dropdown.
4. Select your language.
5. Tap **Save**.
6. When prompted "Restart required", tap **Restart Now**. The app will close and reopen in the new language.

> **Note:** You can also change your language from the first step of the onboarding flow (the Welcome screen).

### Updating Your Profile

1. Tap the settings icon from the main screen.
2. Update any of: name, email, phone number, gender, country, area unit.
3. Tap **Save Settings**.

### Changing Your Location

1. Re-enter the onboarding flow by navigating to Step 6 (Location).
2. Tap **Use GPS** to detect your location automatically, or
3. Tap **Pick on map** to select your farm location manually using the map.

## Feature Reference

### Crop Workflows

| Workflow | Crops | Description |
|----------|-------|-------------|
| Fertilizer recommendation | Cassava, Maize, Sweet potato | Site-specific fertilizer type and quantity |
| Intercrop recommendation | Cassava + Maize, Cassava + Sweet potato | Optimized intercropping fertilizer plan |
| Scheduled planting (SPH) | Cassava | Timing and fertilizer for high-starch production |
| Best planting practices (BPP) | Cassava | Optimal planting window |
| Weed management | Cassava | Weed control method and cost comparison |

### Market & Cost Inputs

| Input | Where entered |
|-------|--------------|
| Cassava market price | Cassava workflow → Market step |
| Maize market price | Maize workflow → Market step |
| Sweet potato market price | Sweet potato workflow → Market step |
| Cassava yield estimate | Cassava workflow → Yield step |
| Tillage cost | Onboarding Step 9 or Tillage workflow |
| Manual labour cost | Manual Tillage Cost screen |
| Tractor availability | Tractor Access screen |

## Known Limitations

| Limitation | Workaround |
|------------|-----------|
| Language change requires full app restart | Tap "Restart Now" when prompted; the restart takes a few seconds |
| Recommendations require internet connection | Ensure connectivity before tapping "Get Recommendation" |
| Dark mode setting is saved but not active | Will be fixed in a future update |
| Past recommendations are not stored | Take a screenshot or note results before leaving the recommendations screen |
| No sharing of recommendations | Screenshot and share manually |
